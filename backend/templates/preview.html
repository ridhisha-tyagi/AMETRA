How Ametra Works

Ametra is a psychological interpretation system built using astrological structure. It does not predict events or define identity. Instead, it translates chart patterns into psychological tendencies — how you process emotion, make decisions, and interact with your internal systems.

The interpretations in this report are generated through structured symbolic mapping, not subjective judgment. They are designed to offer insight, not certainty.

This report is not a diagnosis, and it does not replace professional psychological or medical guidance. It is a tool for reflection — to help you observe patterns, not to limit who you can become.
