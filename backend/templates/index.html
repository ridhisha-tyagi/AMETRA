What is Ametra

Ametra interprets the birth chart as a system of psychological forces. Each placement reflects a pattern — not of events, but of perception, reaction, and internal experience.

Instead of predicting the future, Ametra maps how your identity, emotions, thinking, and behavior interact beneath the surface. It shows you the structure behind your patterns.

What Are Ametra Archetypes?

Ametra archetypes are recurring psychological patterns that shape how you experience yourself and the world around you.

Each archetype reflects a dominant way a person interprets experience, responds to situations, and interacts with others.

They don’t define who you are — they reveal how your inner systems operate, especially under pressure, growth, and change.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Ametra traditional astrology?

No. Ametra does not predict events. It interprets the birth chart as a system of psychological patterns — how you think, feel, and respond.

What information do I need?

You only need your birth date, birth time, and birth location to generate your Ametra Profile.

What will my Ametra Profile show?

Your profile includes your core archetype, growth archetype, psychological system intensity, and a detailed interpretation of the internal dynamics within your chart.

Is the report personalized?

Yes. Each Ametra Profile is generated individually using your birth chart calculations.
